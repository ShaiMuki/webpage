DocAgron

Branding for a documentary film festival at Amir Center, Jerusalem. The Amir Center, or Supersol building, is the most modern and boldest building on the landscape. It was also titled the "ugliest building in Jerusalem" and is the inspiration for this project.

The branding includes a film and event program, posters in two sizes, a popcorn cup, entrance tickets, a website and more.

The project was completed as part of the Container Culture course, led by Michal Sahar.
