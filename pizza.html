Domino's Pizza Games Night

Branding for a new venture at Dominos Pizza: Evening games at the branches. The aim of the initiative is to increase the involvement of Dominos Pizza in the community and make the branch a social gathering place, in contrast to the more common use of the Dominos branch: ordering takeout. The project is part of the Graphic Extreme course, led by Naomi Geiger.

The branding includes four posters for four different game evenings, a redesign of the pizza boxes for both a family pizza and a personal pizza, and an advertising banner.
