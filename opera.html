Posters for the Opera

Design posters for three operas: La Boheme by Giacomo Puccini, Carmen by Georges Bizet, and Arthur Lorenz's suburban story.

I chose to place the operatic story in the streets and alleyways near my apartment in Jerusalem, simply to entertain the question: If these stories were to take place in Israel, in the Jerusalem of 2017, what would they look like?

The project was completed as part of an illustration-design course, led by Orit Bergman.
