An application for Bible study

Making the Bible textbook and the Bible itself neatly accessible in one digital format. The application allows you to be exposed to the entire Bible, to search it in a convenient way, to understand its concepts, read aloud and add content.

The app connects the biblical text to literary and interpretive concepts originating in the textbook, as well as contemporary, scientific and artistic viewpoints.

This project was completed under the guidance of Kobi Levi.
