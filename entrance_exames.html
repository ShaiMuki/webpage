Visual Communication
Admissions Exams

Design of entrance exams in Visual Communication at Bezalel Academy of Art and Design. The challenge was to succeed in creating a memorable, positive and meaningful experience for visual communication candidates at Bezalel, who are likely to be tested in other design schools around the country.

The booklet contains an expression of the architectural experience of the new Bezalel building: the gallery of the Taksim, the Amphitheater, corridors near the Department of Industrial Design, workshops in the art department, the cafeteria and more.
