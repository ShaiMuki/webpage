Al-Hamishmar

A newspaper designed in broadsheet form. In this work, I chose to refer to the historical newspaper Al Hamishmar, whose spirit and pathos reminded me of the kibbutz in which I grew up.

This project was completed as part of a typographic format course, moderated by Theresa Ben Porat.
