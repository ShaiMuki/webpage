Town square

Town square is laying the groundwork for an interactive "newspaper" of discussions, allowing expression in an open, diverse, pluralistic environment that respects and sees the other. The goal of the project is to succeed in generating a reading of current, controversial, broad, diverse and pluralistic issues.

The site consists of user-initiated discussions, and posts published by users in response to the discussion topic. The posts can include, for example, a link to a journalistic article on the Internet, a link to a Wikipedia entry, a tweet from someone on Twitter, a post from Facebook, a newsgroup, and more. It is also possible to respond freely to the discussion.

All the posts in each discussion are prepared according to a "pluralistic code" that aims to give as much space as possible to all position. This editorial exposes the many facets of the issue in discussion and allows users to develop a broad perspective on it.

This project was completed under the guidance of Dana Gez.
